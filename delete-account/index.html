Delete Your Swaralaya Account

If you wish to delete your Swaralaya account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Swaralaya app on your device.
  2. Login to your account using your credentials.
  3. Once logged in, navigate to the Settings page.
  4. Click on the option to delete your account.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm and complete the account deletion process.

Please note that account deletion is a permanent action and cannot be undone.

If you have any questions or encounter any issues during the account deletion process, you can contact our support team at swaralayakaraoke@gmail.com.
